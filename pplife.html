About Forest

Forests are large areas dominated by trees. They are essential for life on Earth. They provide oxygen, shelter, food, and maintain ecological balance.

Types of Forests

Tropical Forest

Hot and wet forests found near the equator with rich biodiversity.

Temperate Forest

Moderate climate forests with seasonal changes.

Boreal Forest

Cold forests found in northern regions, also called taiga.

Forest Animals

Tiger

A powerful predator and symbol of strength.

Elephant

The largest land animal, important for forest ecosystems.

Deer

Graceful herbivores that live in forests.

Importance of Forests

  • Provide Oxygen 🌿
  • Maintain Climate 🌍
  • Home for Wildlife 🐾
  • Prevent Soil Erosion 🌱
